The Edge

Following the incredible response to our previous editions, we've changed things. Along with Edge for early career therapists, we're now launching The Edge for therapists who have 4-6 years of experience as well.

The Edge is designed by therapists, for therapists. The first line in our Kaha manifesto reads: we take our work VERY seriously (Ourselves, not so much). And that is what we aim to bring to you. Get ready for thought-provoking discussions, direct access to our lead therapists, hands-on learning with practical assignments, and an environment where your opinions *matter*. This is your space to grow, and you'll be encouraged to ask questions and take up space. No question is too shallow, and no topic too deep. By the end, you'll walk away feeling confident and READY to become the best version of your therapist self.

Teaching Modules: These 60-90 minute sessions are thoughtfully curated and conducted by expert therapists.   6. Trauma/Queer Informed lens
  7. Crisis and Risk Management
  8. Ethical Dilemmas
  9. Time Keeping and Doorknob Confessions
  10. Termination in Therapy
  11. Schools of thought in action : CBT
  12. Schools of thought in action : DBT

  15. Navigating conversations around payment

FAQs‚ùó‚ùì

You probably have a few questions, so let's answer some of the most common ones:

Will you offer group discounts?

No, we don't. In fact, we'll be addressing the often tricky topic of "money talk" with your clients as well. For starters, feel free to check this out!

Will I get a job with Kaha Mind after this course?

While we can't guarantee a job, we're always on the lookout for the best minds. If you think you fit the bill, write to us at careers@kahamind.com.

I'm still studying; can I apply?

Unfortunately, if you are still pursuing your Undergrad, no. If you are in your final semester of Masters, you can apply for the early career therapists cohort. But basic skills and knowledge are prerequisites for The Edge.

Is this course fully online, in-person, or hybrid?

This cohort will be conducted online. If you're joining us online, all you need is a laptop and a good internet connection. Keeping your video on during sessions is mandatory.

If you're based out of Delhi/Bangalore, we would love for you to drop by at our offices, meet the team and get some extra office hours with us!

Can I record the sessions?

Nice try, but no <3
